About Chitkara Grocery Store

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Chitkara Grocery Store began as a small campus initiative to provide fresh, affordable groceries to students and faculty at Chitkara University. Today, we are a tech-driven grocery service combining AI-powered inventory management, drone deliveries, and farm-to-table freshness.

Our Mission

We are revolutionizing grocery shopping with AI-driven recommendations, zero-waste packaging, and hyper-local sourcing. Our vision is to make sustainable, high-quality groceries accessible to everyone while reducing food waste by 50% by 2025.
